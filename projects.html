What We're Actually Doing

Coral Reefs

The reefs are bleaching and dying. We're trying to regrow them by planting coral and protecting the ones that are left. It's slow work but it matters.

Plastic Cleanup

There's too much plastic in the ocean. We organize cleanups and pull it out. We also tell companies to stop making so much of it. One of those works better than the other.

Marine Life

Fish are being overfished. Some species are nearly gone. We fight illegal fishing and push for protected areas where nothing can be killed. It's working, sort of.
