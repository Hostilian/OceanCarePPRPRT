Frequently Asked Questions

Where does my donation go?

We publish detailed monthly impact reports. Every dollar is tracked and verified by independent auditors. See our transparency dashboard.

Do I need experience to volunteer?

No! We have opportunities for all skill levels. Many of our volunteers are beginners. Training provided for all activities.

What happens after I report debris?

Our team reviews the report within 24 hours and assesses cleanup feasibility. You'll receive updates on our coordinated cleanup effort.

Is my personal information secure?

Yes. We use 256-bit SSL encryption and never sell personal data. See our privacy policy for complete details.
