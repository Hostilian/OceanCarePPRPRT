OceanCare Paper Prototype Walkthrough

This Gemini scenario shows how Alex, Maria, and John move through the OceanCare mobile prototype. Each sketch mimics the physical paper screens you can print, cut, and use in a moderated study for the Human Computer Interaction course.

Personas, Scenarios, and HCI Alignment

The walkthrough covers three primary user goals drawn from the Moodle CZU brief: Alex registers for a weekend cleanup, Maria files a debris report in the moment, and John donates to the Save the Turtles campaign. The imagery demonstrates the facilitator flow with overlay cards so you can rehearse task prompts and success criteria.

Task 1 · Step 1 — Home Screen to Events

Alex taps Find Events from the home hub to move toward available cleanups.

Task 1 · Step 2 — Confirm Cleanup Spot

The Join overlay confirms the Ocean Beach Cleanup registration with a single tap.

Task 2 · Step 1 — Start Marine Debris Report

Maria opens the Report Issue screen and prepares to capture location, photo, and description.

Task 2 · Step 2 — Submit Confirmation

The completed form highlights the Submit Report button and the follow up success card.

Task 3 · Step 1 — Choose Donation Amount

John selects the $20 preset for the Save the Turtles campaign before paying.

Task 3 · Step 2 — Thank You Overlay

A gratitude card confirms the $20 gift and guides John back to the main flow.

Facilitator Tip: Layer each pop up panel with a separate index card or sticky so you can physically drop it on top during the session.

Task Goals: Keep the narration simple: Help Alex finish a registration, capture Maria's field report in under a minute, and close John's donation with reassurance.

Assessment: Mark success when the participant explains what happens next without extra coaching. Capture friction points for iteration.
