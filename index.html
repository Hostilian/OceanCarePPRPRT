Our Mission

OceanCare Initiative was founded on the belief that direct action is the most effective way to combat the threats facing our oceans. From plastic pollution to overfishing and climate change, the challenges are immense, but so is our resolve.

We organize large-scale clean-up operations, fund critical research, advocate for policy change, and empower local communities to become stewards of their marine environments. Our approach is holistic, science-based, and driven by a passionate global community.
