Our Mission

The OceanCare Initiative is dedicated to protecting and restoring the world's oceans. We work through research, community engagement, conservation projects, and education to combat ocean pollution, preserve marine life, and promote sustainable practices.

From coral reef restoration to marine debris cleanup, we are committed to creating lasting positive change for ocean health and the communities that depend on it.
