Frequently Asked Questions

📄 Is my donation tax-deductible? ▼

Yes! OceanCare Initiative is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit. Your donation is tax-deductible to the full extent allowed by law. Consult a tax professional for details.

💬 Can I see where my money goes? ▼

Absolutely! Your donor dashboard shows real-time updates on your impact. You'll receive monthly impact reports on your contributions.

🔄 Can I set up recurring donations? ▼

Yes! Visit your donor dashboard to set up monthly or yearly recurring donations. Monthly givers receive a special impact report and exclusive updates.
