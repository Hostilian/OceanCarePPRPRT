Project Brief

Website Goal & Mission

OceanCare Initiative is a non-profit platform focused on awareness, transparent reporting, and reliable donation funnels. The homepage must immediately share what we do, why it matters, and how every visitor can help today.

Goal: Create a website to raise awareness and gather donations for ocean protection while keeping volunteer opportunities and debris reporting one click away.

Homepage Requirements

  • Mission overview and recent news
  • Interactive donation calculator with transparent impact
  • Volunteer pathways and CTAs
  • Live ocean data & climate trends
  • Donor dashboard teaser for logged-in supporters
  • Report debris workflow + project/project pages

Our Mission

OceanCare Initiative is a non-profit organization dedicated to protecting marine ecosystems through conservation, education, and community action.

With real-time environmental data, volunteer coordination tools, and verified impact tracking, we're building a transparent ocean conservation platform.

Our Core Values:

  • Transparency - Verified impact reporting
  • Accessibility - Free tools for all
  • Science-Driven - Real-time ocean data
  • Community First - Powered by volunteers
