A: Yes! We use industry-standard encryption and security 
practices. Your personal information is never shared.

**Q: Can I volunteer remotely?**
A: You can view cleanup opportunities and report debris from 
anywhere. Physical cleanup is done locally in coastal communities.

**Q: How is my donation used?**
A: Donations fund ocean cleanup equipment, volunteer training, 
and platform maintenance. See our transparency report for details.

A: We use 8 free APIs: GNews (news), Open-Meteo (weather),

Crossing (climate trends).

A: Yes! The website is fully responsive and works on phones, 
tablets, and computers.
